Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PennyMate

What is PennyMate?
PennyMate is a personal finance management application designed to simplify tracking of income, expenses, and budgets. It allows you to record transactions, create and modify monthly/annual budgets, and generate detailed reports with charts.
How do I add a new transaction?
To add a new transaction:
  1. Click on the "+" button in the Transactions section
  2. Fill in the transaction details (category, date, type, amount, description)
  3. Click the checkmark button to confirm
  4. The transaction will be added to your records and reflected in your budget
How do I set or change my budget?
You can set budgets for weekly, monthly, and annual periods:
  1. Go to the respective section (Weekly, Monthly, or Annual Statistics)
  2. Click the edit (pencil) button
  3. Enter your new budget amount
  4. Click the checkmark to confirm or the X to cancel
Your remaining budget will be automatically calculated based on your transactions.
How do I view my financial analytics?
Click on the "Analytics" tab in the sidebar menu to view various charts including:
  • Pie charts showing expense categories
  • Line charts tracking spending over time
  • Bar charts comparing income and expenses
These visualizations help you understand your spending patterns at a glance.
Can I change the currency?
Yes, you can change the currency in the Budget Settings:
  1. Click the gear icon in the Budget section
  2. Select your preferred currency from the dropdown menu (EUR, USD, GBP)
  3. Confirm your selection
Note that this doesn't convert existing amounts, it only changes the displayed currency symbol.
How do I edit my profile information?
To update your profile:
  1. Go to "Edit Profile" in the sidebar menu
  2. Update your name, email, and/or password
  3. Click "Save Changes" to confirm
  4. If you don't want to change your password, leave the password field blank
Is my financial data secure?
Yes, PennyMate takes security seriously:
  • All data is encrypted using advanced security measures
  • Regular backups are performed to prevent data loss
  • The system is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure
  • We implement continuous monitoring and firewall protection
However, you should always use a strong, unique password for your account.
Why do I see advertisements?
PennyMate is currently free to use, and advertisements help support the development and maintenance of the application. We strive to keep ads non-intrusive and relevant. If you have feedback about the advertising experience, please let us know through the support page.
